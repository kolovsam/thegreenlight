Co-Working

Co-working or body doubling can help get difficult tasks started and increase creativity and focus. These events are intended to bring together small business owners and entrepreurs to help push through challenges, potentially collaborate, and support one another. Remote workers, freelancers, and others who work alone or who could otherwise benefit from this set up are welcome!
